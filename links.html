Links that I like:

  • Vic Firth 40 Essential Rudiments : the first drumming website I spent hours on. recently been revisiting to learn the ones I avoided
  • "On Loneliness: Luxury and punishment at the end of the world" by Hannah Baer : really beautiful essay on the juxtaposition of loneliness as a luxury and punishment that I always find myself coming back to. was really helpful during long bouts of isolation
  • My Edge Is Anything But Straight: Towards A Radical Queer Critique Of Intoxication Culture : well researched and empowering zine on being queer and sober (kind of hard to read and organize, but you can imagine all of the pages stacked such that first page is the bottom of the stack and the left side is the first half page and the right side is the last half page, if that makes any sense)
