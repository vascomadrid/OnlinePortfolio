some random links I like

  • Vic Firth 40 Essential Rudiments : the first drumming website I spent hours on. recently been revisiting to learn the ones I missed as a child
  • "On Loneliness: Luxury and punishment at the end of the world" by Hannah Baer : really beautiful essay on loneliness and queerness and wealth and prisons and more, find myself coming back to it often
  • The Metal Archives: I like that this website exists
  • My Edge Is Anything But Straight: Towards A Radical Queer Critique Of Intoxication Culture : well researched and hopeful zine on being queer and sober (kind of hard to read online)
  • Baked Chicken Breast Recipe : I like the way the chicken came out when I followed this recipe, though I was a little more generous with the spices
  • Rediscovering the hidden gems of Venezuela’s musical avant-garde. : music is a great way to connect with the culture you have been stripped from, cool article and compilation
  • Club Penguin Journey : club penguin remake that I really love and spend maybe too much time on
  • INTO ANOTHER WORLD: AN INTERVIEW WITH TRHÄ : damian ojeda fascinates me as a musician with a lot of output, cool interview
