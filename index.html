Recommended Reading

  • THE FANTASY WORLD MASTER LIST OF RESOURCES ON HOW TO DISMANTLE SYSTEMIC RACISM By exploring the drop down menu for literature you can find links to resources on black revolutionaries, black history, lateral violence, freedom, etc. The PFW guides section has guides available directly on the website on topics like resolving issues without the police, defunding the police, etc.
  • YURUGU by MARIMBA ANI Comprehensive textbook critiquing the entirety of european cultural thought and behavior. Highly recommended for anyone interested in learning about the traits of ancient to modern philosophy that have perpetuated systemic racism in the collective thought of western culture and language.
  • ZAMI: A NEW SPELLING OF MY NAME, A Biomythography by Audre Lorde
